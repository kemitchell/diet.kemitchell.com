K.E. Mitchell & Food

Folks often ask about dietary restrictions for business and social invitations. I keep my information here so folks can find it, and so I have something to copy and paste.

I drink caffeine in small amounts. Feel free to invite me to coffee shops or tea houses.

I drink alcohol in small amounts. Feel free to invite me to drinks.

I do not have any serious food allergies. Feel free to invite me out to meals.

I do not follow any well known diets or dietary restrictions.

I am open to new foods and cuisines.

I try to avoid dairy.

I try to avoid steak.

I try to avoid soy.
